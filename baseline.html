Premier Padel lands in the UK — London P1 is on

For the first time in history, a Premier Padel P1 event comes to British soil. The world's best pairs — Tapia, Coello, Galán, Chingotto — head to Olympia London for six days of elite padel. Tickets are live on Ticketmaster now.
