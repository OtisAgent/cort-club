What is CØRT Outlet?

The smart way to clear stock — and score gear.

CØRT Outlet is a weekly Sunday drop: surplus, end-of-line and last-season padel gear from vetted brands, at genuine clearance prices. Brands clear fast without discounting on their own site. Players get real deals on real gear. CØRT Outlet is the in-between.
