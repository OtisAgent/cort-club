Agreement

The terms on which brands and sellers list and sell their products through the CØRT Shop.

This agreement is between CØRT and you, the Vendor. It sets out how selling through CØRT works: you keep and ship your own stock, CØRT lists, markets and sells it to the padel community, and CØRT takes a commission on each sale. Applying to join, or listing a product, means you accept these terms.

1Who this is between

This agreement is between CORT CLUB LTD, company number 17325437, registered at 33 Vicarage Road, Sheffield, S35 8RF (trading as "CØRT", "we", "us"), and you, the business applying to sell through CØRT (the "Vendor", "you"). CORT Club Ltd is not VAT registered.

2How selling through CØRT works

You list your products on the CØRT Shop. When a customer buys, the sale is made through CØRT, but you hold the stock and ship it directly to the customer. CØRT provides and runs the storefront, checkout, marketing and first-line customer contact. This is a marketplace arrangement — it does not make us partners, an employer, or agents of each other beyond what's set out here.

3Commission & fees

CØRT charges a commission of 10% — our founding-brand rate — on the Product Price of each sale made through CØRT. "Product Price" means the price the customer pays for the product itself, excluding delivery charges. CØRT is not VAT registered, so no VAT is added to our commission.

  • There are no listing fees, joining fees or monthly fees — commission is the only charge.
  • Commission applies to every completed sale. If a sale is later refunded or cancelled, the commission on it is reversed too.
  • We may change the commission rate on [30] days' written notice; if you don't accept the new rate you may end this agreement before it takes effect.

4Payments & payouts

CØRT collects the full amount the customer pays. We retain our commission and pay the balance (the Product Price and any delivery you charged, less commission and less any refunds) to you.

Payouts are made [monthly, within 30 days of the end of the calendar month] in which the item was dispatched, to the bank details you provide. You are responsible for your own tax and VAT on your sales.

5Products, pricing & listings

You provide accurate product information, images and prices, and keep stock levels up to date. You set your own selling prices. We may format, present or lightly edit listings to fit the CØRT store, and we may decline or remove any product at our discretion (for example if it's out of stock, mispriced, or not a fit for the community).

You confirm you have the right to sell every product you list and to use all images and content you supply.

6Orders & dispatch

When an order comes in, we pass you the details and you dispatch the product directly to the customer, within [2 working days], with tracking where available. You keep the customer's data only to fulfil that order — you must not add CØRT customers to your own marketing or sell to them directly outside CØRT.

7Returns, refunds & faulty goods

Because the customer buys through CØRT, we are the seller in the eyes of the customer and we handle the customer-facing side of returns and refunds. As between you and us:

  • Change-of-mind returns (the customer's 14-day right): the returned item comes back to you, and the commission on that sale is reversed.
  • Faulty, damaged or not-as-described items: you bear the cost — you refund or replace, cover return postage, and reimburse CØRT for any refund we've issued the customer on your behalf.
  • You warrant your products are as described, of satisfactory quality and fit for purpose.

8Product safety, legality & your promises

You confirm and warrant that everything you sell through CØRT:

  • is genuine, safe, and complies with all applicable UK laws, safety and labelling rules;
  • is not counterfeit and does not infringe anyone's intellectual property or other rights;
  • is accurately described, and legally yours to sell.

You will indemnify CØRT against any claims, losses, fines or costs arising from your products, your descriptions, or your breach of this agreement — including product-liability and IP claims.

9Brand & intellectual property

You keep ownership of your brand, trade marks and product content. You grant CØRT a licence to use your name, logo, images and product content to list, market and promote your products across the CØRT store and channels while this agreement is in place. CØRT keeps ownership of the CØRT brand and platform.

10Data protection

Each of us will comply with UK data protection law. Customer personal data shared with you is strictly for fulfilling orders. You must not use it for your own marketing or pass it to anyone else.

11Term, suspension & ending the agreement

This agreement runs until either of us ends it. Either party may end it on [30] days' written notice. We may suspend or remove your listings immediately if you breach these terms, if there's a safety or legal concern, or to protect customers or the CØRT brand.

On ending, any orders already placed must still be fulfilled, and any commission or payouts owed are settled in the normal cycle.

12Liability

Neither party excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, for fraud, or for anything that can't be excluded by law. Otherwise, neither party is liable to the other for loss of profit or indirect losses, and each party's total liability is limited to [the commission paid/earned in the previous 12 months] — except for your indemnity obligations above, which are not capped.

13General

This is the whole agreement between us on this subject. We may update these terms on reasonable notice. Nothing here creates a partnership, joint venture or employment. This agreement is governed by the law of England & Wales, and the courts of England & Wales have jurisdiction.
