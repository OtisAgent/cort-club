6Your 14-day right to change your mind

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, if you're a consumer buying online you can cancel most orders within 14 days — for any reason — without giving a reason.

  • The 14 days start the day after you receive the goods (or the last item, if your order arrives in parts).
  • Tell us you want to cancel within that window (email admin@cortclub.co.uk).
  • Send the goods back within 14 days of telling us, unused and in a resaleable condition.
  • We'll refund you within 14 days of getting the goods back (or your proof of return), including the standard delivery cost you originally paid.
  • You pay the cost of returning the item, unless it's faulty or not as described.

A few things can't be cancelled this way — for example hygiene-sealed items (such as socks) once the seal is broken, or personalised items. We'll always make this clear on the product page.

7Faulty or misdescribed items

This is separate from, and on top of, your 14-day right above. Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015, everything you buy from us must be of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose and as described. If it isn't:

  • Within 30 days — you can reject it and get a full refund.
  • After 30 days — you're entitled to a repair or replacement, and a refund if that isn't possible.

If something's faulty, we cover the cost of returning it. This applies equally to partner brand items — we sort it out with you directly.
